Privacy policy

Overview

We do not collect, hold, use or disclose personal information to carry out functions. Our app is stateless and discards all data after each request. logging info on errors and usage rates is maintain to aid in debugging but not disclosed. aggregated usage statistics are kept by google as part of the google cloud services platform.
